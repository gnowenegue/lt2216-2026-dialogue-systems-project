# Project presentations
Each project gets 15 minutes total, divided roughly as follows:

1. Presentation (about 5 mins)
2. Demo (about 5 mins)
3. Discussion with audience (4-5 mins)

The presentation should ideally cover the following (a suggestion is to make 1 slide on each topic and present each slide in roughly 1 minute):

- The game: what does it do?
- Technicalities: what platform, which standards etc were used?
- Challenges; what was difficult and how did you solve it?
- Relation to course contents;
  - What parts of the course were most useful to you, and how did you use them?
  - Was statecharts (if you used that) a suitable implementation framework for your project? why (not)? Do you think some other framework would have been more suitable?
  - Briefly describe your development process. How did you use input from peer reviewers in the development process?
  - Are there any ethical concerns regarding your project?
- Future work: how could the game and its conversational features be developed?
